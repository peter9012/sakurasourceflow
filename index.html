Our Story

Our Heritage in Woodcraft

Mastering the Art of Sustainable Wooden Products Since 1996

Founded in the heart of China's craftsmanship region, Sakura Sourceflow has evolved from a small family workshop to a leader in premium wooden tableware. Our journey reflects our unwavering commitment to quality, sustainability, and timeless design.

Today, we operate a state-of-the-art manufacturing facility specializing in bamboo and wooden disposable products, complemented by a network of over 1,000 trusted partner factories. This unique structure allows us to combine artisanal techniques with modern efficiency.
