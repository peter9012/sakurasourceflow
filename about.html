Our Journey

Since 1985

Humble Beginnings

Founded by master craftsman Liang Wei in a small workshop in Hangzhou, Sakura Sourceflow began with a simple vision: to create beautiful wooden products that honored both nature and tradition.

What started as a family business crafting traditional Chinese wooden tableware has grown into an internationally recognized brand, while maintaining our commitment to artisanal quality.

2005 Breakthrough

The Sustainable Turn

In 2005, we made the pivotal decision to transition to 100% sustainable materials and processes, becoming one of the first woodcraft companies in Asia to achieve FSC certification.

This commitment transformed our business model and established Sakura Sourceflow as a leader in eco-conscious manufacturing without compromising on design excellence.
